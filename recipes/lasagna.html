lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics

Ingrediens

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • One bottle spaghetti sauce
  • A block of Cheese
  • 3 Eggs
  • One tbs of dried parsely
  • One tbs of salt
  • One tbs of black pepper
  • One bag Lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 a cup of water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the chicken breasts, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, block of cheese , 3 eggs and all the seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 mins
  5. Uncover and continue to bake for 10 mins
